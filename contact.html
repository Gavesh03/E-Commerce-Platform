Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery options?

We offer island-wide delivery within 2-5 business days. Free shipping is available for orders over LKR 50,000. Express delivery is available for Colombo and suburbs.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unopened products. Defective items can be returned within 30 days for replacement or refund.

Do you offer warranty?

All our products come with manufacturer warranty. Warranty periods vary by product and brand. Extended warranty options are available for most items.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash on delivery. Installment payment options are available through selected banks.
