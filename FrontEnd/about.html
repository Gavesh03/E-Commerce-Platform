Our Story

Founded in 2016, LAPZO has grown from a small computer repair shop in Colombo to Sri Lanka's leading destination for premium computers, laptops, and tech accessories.

Our mission is simple: to provide our customers with the latest technology at competitive prices, backed by exceptional customer service and expert technical support.

With over 10 years of experience in the industry, we've built strong relationships with top brands like Apple, Dell, HP, ASUS, and more, ensuring that our customers always have access to genuine, high-quality products.
